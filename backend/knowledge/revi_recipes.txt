NAKAI Matcha Recipes

NAKAI organic matcha is available in multiple grades. Below are recommended recipes to enjoy NAKAI matcha to its fullest. For ceremonial preparations (usucha and koicha), we recommend 二十二 NIJYU-NI (22) Ceremonial Reserved or 四 SHI (4) Specialty Grade. For lattes and mixed drinks, any NAKAI matcha works beautifully.

Usucha (Thin Tea):
- Use 2g (2 scoops) of matcha
- Heat water to 75-80°C (not boiling)
- Pour 70ml of water into a pre-warmed bowl
- Whisk briskly in an M or W pattern for 15 seconds until a fine foam forms
- Ceremonial grades like 二十二 (22) shine brightest in thin tea, revealing their full umami and creamy finish

Koicha (Thick Tea):
- Use 4g (4 scoops) of matcha
- Heat water to 75-80°C
- Pour 40ml of water into a pre-warmed bowl
- Slowly knead (do not whisk) in a circular motion until smooth and thick
- Best with 二十二 NIJYU-NI (22) — its rich umami and velvety body are fully expressed in koicha

Matcha Latte:
- Sift 2g of matcha into a cup
- Add 30ml of hot water (75-80°C) and whisk until dissolved
- Steam or heat 200ml of your preferred milk (oat milk pairs beautifully)
- Pour milk over the matcha concentrate
- Optional: sweeten lightly with honey or agave
- 十六 JU-ROKU (16) and 十七 JU-NANA (17) are excellent choices for lattes

Iced Matcha:
- Sift 2g of matcha into a glass
- Add 30ml of hot water (75-80°C) and whisk until smooth
- Fill glass with ice
- Pour 200ml of cold water or cold milk over ice
- Stir gently

Matcha Affogato:
- Prepare a shot of usucha (2g matcha + 30ml hot water, whisked)
- Place a scoop of vanilla ice cream in a bowl
- Pour the hot matcha over the ice cream
- Enjoy immediately for the contrast of hot and cold

Tips for the best NAKAI matcha experience:
- Always sift the matcha to avoid clumps
- Use water at 75-80°C — boiling water makes matcha bitter
- Use a bamboo chasen (tea whisk) for the best results — NAKAI offers the Takayama Chasen 100-prong whisk
- Store opened matcha in an airtight container in the refrigerator
- Use within 2-4 weeks of opening for optimal freshness and flavor
