NAKAI Matcha Shipping and Returns Policy

SHIPPING INFORMATION:
- NAKAI ships worldwide to over 40 countries including USA, Canada, UK, EU, Japan, Australia, and more.
- Free shipping is available on qualifying orders.
- Orders are processed within 1-3 business days.
- Standard shipping typically takes 5-10 business days depending on destination.
- Express shipping options are available at checkout.
- All orders include tracking information sent via email.
- Packages are carefully sealed to preserve matcha freshness and quality.

RETURN POLICY:
- We have an 8-day return policy. You can request a return within 8 days of receiving your item.
- Items must be in the same condition as when you received them (unused, with tags, in original packaging).
- A receipt or proof of purchase is required.
- To initiate a return, contact us at contact@nakaiinfo.com.
- Returns must be sent to the address provided during the return process.
- Items returned without prior authorization will not be accepted.

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS:
- Please inspect your order upon receipt.
- If you notice any defects, damages, or incorrect items, contact us immediately so we can evaluate the issue and resolve it promptly.

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS:
- Items used for more than five days cannot be returned.

EXCHANGES:
- The fastest way to get what you want is to return the original item and place a new order for the desired product.
