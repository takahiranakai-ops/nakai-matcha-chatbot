NAKAI Grading System:
- SS Grade Plus: The highest quality tier. Ultra-fine stone-ground matcha from first harvest shade-grown tencha. Deep umami, creamy body, and complex flavor. REVI is SS Grade Plus.
- SS Grade: Premium quality. Smooth, balanced flavor with vibrant green color. Great for both drinking straight and in lattes. IKIGAI is SS Grade.
- Both grades are 100% organic, JAS-certified, and sourced from Japan's finest tea-growing regions.

Which NAKAI matcha is right for you:
- For traditional tea ceremony (usucha or koicha): REVI (SS Grade Plus) is the ideal choice for its depth and complexity.
- For daily matcha drinking: Both REVI and IKIGAI are excellent. IKIGAI offers exceptional value for everyday enjoyment.
- For matcha lattes and drinks: Both work beautifully. IKIGAI is popular for lattes. REVI adds a premium touch.
- For a gift or first-time experience: The Exquisite Matcha Set lets you experience both grades.
- For health and wellness: Both grades are rich in L-theanine, EGCG, and antioxidants.
