Preparation Guide

Usucha (Thin Tea):
Sift 2g of matcha into a bowl. Add 70ml of water at 75-80 degrees Celsius (never boiling — it destroys L-theanine). Whisk vigorously in an M or W pattern for 15 seconds until a fine, creamy foam forms. The foam should be uniform with tiny, tight bubbles.

Koicha (Thick Tea) — REVI recommended:
Sift 4g of matcha into a bowl. Add 40ml of water at 75 degrees Celsius. Instead of whisking, knead slowly in circular motions until the matcha becomes a thick, paint-like consistency. Koicha reveals the deepest expression of the matcha's character — it should taste rich, sweet, and completely smooth.

Matcha Latte:
Sift 2g of matcha into a small bowl or cup. Add 30ml of hot water (80 degrees Celsius) and whisk until smooth. Pour into a glass and add 200ml of steamed or cold milk (oat milk recommended). For iced lattes, pour the whisked matcha over ice and cold milk.

Storage:
Keep in an airtight container, refrigerated after opening. Use within 30 days of opening for optimal freshness. Unopened, store in a cool, dark place away from strong odors.
