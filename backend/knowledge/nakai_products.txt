About NAKAI

NAKAI is a specialty organic matcha brand founded in 2024 by Takahira Nakai, headquartered in Dubai, UAE. The brand philosophy is "grounded in nature, elevated in ritual." Every NAKAI matcha is 100% organic, JAS + USDA certified, hand-picked from first harvest (ichibancha), and stone-ground to a particle size of 5-10 micrometers. NAKAI sources exclusively from certified organic farms in Kagoshima (Kirishima) and Kyoto (Uji), Japan. Tea selection is advised by Haruhide Morita, a 3-time champion of Japan's National Tea Quality Appraisal Competition (1970, 1981, 1992). Each matcha is identified by a number, each with its own story, terroir, cultivar, and character. Note: NAKAI is a distinct brand from Nakai Organic Tea Garden (Wazuka, Kyoto).

Preparation Guide

Usucha (Thin Tea):
Sift 2g of matcha into a bowl. Add 70ml of water at 75-80 degrees Celsius (never boiling — it destroys L-theanine). Whisk vigorously in an M or W pattern for 15 seconds until a fine, creamy foam forms.

Koicha (Thick Tea) — 二十二 recommended:
Sift 4g of matcha into a bowl. Add 40ml of water at 75 degrees Celsius. Instead of whisking, knead slowly in circular motions until the matcha becomes a thick, paint-like consistency. Koicha reveals the deepest expression of the matcha's character.

Matcha Latte:
Sift 2g of matcha into a small bowl or cup. Add 30ml of hot water (80 degrees Celsius) and whisk until smooth. Pour into a glass and add 200ml of steamed or cold milk (oat milk recommended).

Water-only preparation (二十二 recommended):
Prepare as usucha, but use slightly cooler water (70-75 degrees) to let the matcha's clarity come into focus. This is where 二十二's character shines most.

Storage:
Keep in an airtight container, refrigerated after opening. Use within 30 days of opening for optimal freshness. Unopened, store in a cool, dark place away from strong odors.

Matcha Science

L-theanine (~45mg per 2g serving):
An amino acid unique to tea. Promotes alpha brain wave activity — calm, focused alertness without drowsiness. Works synergistically with caffeine for sustained cognitive performance. NAKAI matcha contains approximately 15x more L-theanine than standard steeped green tea due to shade cultivation.

EGCG (137x more than steeped green tea):
The most studied catechin in green tea. Matcha delivers 137x more EGCG because you consume the entire leaf. Extensive research links EGCG to antioxidant protection and cellular health.

Caffeine (~35mg per 2g serving):
Less than coffee (~95mg) but more sustained. L-theanine modulates caffeine absorption, creating a 4-6 hour energy curve with no jitters and no crash.

Particle size (5-15 micrometers):
The human tongue detects grit at 25 micrometers — NAKAI matcha is well below this threshold, ensuring silky smooth texture.
